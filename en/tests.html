Wisconsin Card Sorting Test (WCST)

This is a somewhat unusual test because you need to match each card given to you with one of the four key cards. I cannot tell you how to match the cards, but I will tell you each time whether your match is "right" or "wrong." There is no time limit for this test, so you do not need to rush.
