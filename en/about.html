About Me

"Hello, I am Dr. Abdulkadir Ermiş. I completed my specialization as a research assistant in the field of neurology at Istanbul Medipol University, Faculty of Medicine, Department of Neurology. I work especially on neuroimmunology and neurodegenerative diseases such as Multiple Sclerosis and Alzheimer's Disease."

My Mission

"In my clinical practice and research, I observe the profound impact of cognitive health on overall quality of life through our patients every day. I launched the DEEP BRAIN project to help people proactively protect their brain health by bringing scientifically based and accessible mind exercises to a wider audience."

My Vision

"My goal is to develop and protect cognitive functions instead of lazy minds and limited neuronal reserve with DEEP BRAIN, not just as a gaming platform. "To make it a scientifically based platform that anyone who wants to can use."
